  • Tap to pay

    Tap to pay is a payment technology that allows users to make contactless payments by simply tapping their payment device (such as a credit card, smartphone, or smartwatch) on a payment terminal such as PocketPos.

  • Online ordering

    Mobile app online ordering allows customers to browse menus, place orders, and pay using a mobile device. Businesses receive and process orders, with the option for delivery or pickup. It's a convenient way for customers to order and for businesses to streamline operations.

  • Kiosk ordering

    Kiosk ordering is a self-service ordering system that allows customers to place orders through a touch-screen kiosk or tablet. This method of ordering has become increasingly popular in recent years, particularly for food and retail businesses.
